What We Do

The Society of Women Engineers is a non-profit educational and service organization that empowers women to aspire to careers in engineering and thrive as leaders in society.



Executive Board

Executive Board manages Columbia chapter of SWE.

Marketing

Chair: Nina Sabharwal

As the marketing committee, our aim is to keep SWE’s name alive. Through social media, namely Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter, we make sure everyone is aware of SWE and our activities. Also, with our SWE swap designs (such as t-shirts, flyers, and banners) we rejuvenate the SWE spirit!




Engineering Exploration Experience

Chair : Colette McCullagh & Minah Kim

Engineering Exploration Experience holds an annual event inviting 50+ high schools to Columbia, for a day filled with wet labs, lectures, mentorship, and a brief introduction to life as an engineering student. We are extending our efforts to reach out to younger students. Starting Fall 2013, we will be creating mentorship programs and introducing exposure to engineering to middle school students.

Corporate

Chair : Jessica Zhao

We plan events with companies seeking to hire female engineers. Our events strive to connect professionals with students for job and internship opportunities, mentorship, and professional development.




Networking

Chair : Leslie Hill

Speed networking is an extensively planned networking event held early on spring semester. SWE partners with the Center for Career Education to invite over 50 alumni to spend an evening with over 100 Columbia students in which they share stories about career choices, college experiences, and much more over delicious refreshments. The format mirrors speed dating for the first part with timed rounds and proceeds in a freer from networking events in the last hour.

Membership/Mentorship

Chair : Erica Yee & Victoria Lee

The purpose of membership/mentorship is to organize events to increase camaraderie between the women engineering community and to facilitate a mentorship program between upperclassmen and underclassmen.




Faculty and Alumni

Chair: Andrea Ortuño

The SWE Faculty/Alumni Committee enables Columbia engineering students to connect with and learn from engineering professors and past students. Through casual events such as dinners and mixers, we hope to expand the Columbia engineering network and foster long-lasting relationships.

Community Outreach

Chair : Liz Dente

The objective of SWE Outreach is to expose female students to the exciting career opportunities offered by all branches of engineering, both in academia and in industry. We want to show these students the excitement and challenges that engineers face in their daily lives, and more importantly the integral roles that engineers fulfill in our world today.


Our MISSION

is to promote and encourage women in the Engineering and Sciences field and to emphasize the importance of higher education as well as maintaining perseverance in pursuing leadership and professional distinction among society.
